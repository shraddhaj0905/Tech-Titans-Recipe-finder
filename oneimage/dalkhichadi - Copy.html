DAL-KHICHADI

Ingredients



  • ½ cup rice, ½ cup moong dal
  • 1 tsp ghee, ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp salt, 3¼ cup water
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely choppedr

Recipe


Step-1

Firstly, in a large bowl rinse and soak ½ cup rice, ½ cup moong dal for 10 minutes..


Step-2

In a cooker heat 1 tsp ghee and add soaked dal and rice.Saute for 2 minutes, or until the dal turns aromatic.


Step-3

Now add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp salt and 3¼ cup water. mix well.Cover and pressure cook for 5 minutes.In a large kadai heat 2 tbsp ghee. add 1 tsp cumin, 1 bay leaf and pinch hing.


Step-4

Saute on low flame until the spices turn aromaticNow add 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and saute well.Further, add 1 tomato and saute until the tomato turns soft and mushy.


Step-5

Keep the flame on low add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp garam masala and ½ tsp salt.Saute for 2 minutes or until the spices turn aromatic.


Step-6

Now add cooked rice and dal. Also, add 1 cup water and mix well adjusting consistency as required.


Step-7

Cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until the flavours are absorbed well.


Step-8

finally, serve MIX VEGETABLE CURRY


Step-9

inally, add 2 tbsp coriander and enjoy dal khichdi with pickle and curd.
