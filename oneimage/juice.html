JUICE

Ingredients



  • 1 cup ripe papaya cubed
  • 1 riped banana
  • 4 tbsp mango finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp apple finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp black grapes
  • 4 tbsp black grapes
  • 4 tbsp pineapple finely chopped I didn’t add
  • 1 tbsp nanari syrup optional
  • ½ cup water
  • Few ice cubes
  • Add little sugar

Recipe


Step-1

In a bowl add the banana. Mash this well using a fork. Do not puree this in a mixie.


Step-2

In a mixie jar add the papaya cubes and add ½ cup of water.


Step-3

Now add the apple, mango, pineapple and grapes.


Step-4

add 1 tbsp of sugar if needed. Now add in the ice cubes. Mix well.


Step-5

Pour this into the serving glass.
