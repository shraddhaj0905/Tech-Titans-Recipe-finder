DABELI

Ingredients



  • 3 potato / aloo, boiled & mashed
  • 2 tbsp spiced peanu
  • ¾ teaspoon salt, or as needed
  • 1 tsp aamchur
  • 1 ¼ cups water, or more as needed
  • 2 tbsp pomegranate
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon mild chile powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 pinch ground ginger
  • 5 tsp onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more to taste

Recipe


For Dabeli Masala:

Roast red chili, coriander seeds, cinnamon sticks, cloves, and cumin seeds in a nonstick pan until fragrant. Grind the roasted spices into a fine powder.


For Dabeli Mixture:

Heat peanut oil in a nonstick pan, add cumin seeds. Once they crackle, add hing water. Add chopped onion, dabeli masala, and tamarind chutney. Cook for a while. Add salt, boiled potatoes, and cook for another 1-2 minutes. Mix in chopped coriander, masala peanut, pomegranate seeds, and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add chutney, chopped coriander, butter, grated coconut, and cook for 2-3 more minutes. Set aside to cool.


For Chutney:

Grind soaked red chilies and garlic cloves into a fine paste. Add lemon juice, salt, and mix well.


For Finishing

Slit the pavs in half and grill them on a nonstick pan with butter. Spread dabeli masala on both sides of each pav and sprinkle with salt. Turn off the flame. Spread meethi chutney on one side and tikhi chutney on the other side of each pav. Spread dabeli mixture on one side of the pav. Top with masala peanut, pomegranate seeds, nylon sev, and butter. Serve hot, garnished with more masala peanut, pomegranate seeds, nylon sev, chutney, and chopped coriander.
