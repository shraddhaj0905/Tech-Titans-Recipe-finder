PALAK-PANNER

Ingredients



  • 500 gms Spinach, pureed
  • 2 tbsp Oil1, tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 Bay leaf ,1 tsp Ginger finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Garlic,1 cup Onion paste (boiled)
  • 1/2 cup Tomato pureed,2 tsp Salt
  • 2-3 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp Garam masala
  • 2 tbsp of ghee or oil
  • 1 tbsp Cream

Recipe


Step-1

Boil spinach in a pressure cooker and grind it to make palak puree.Palak Paneer2.Heat oil in a pan and fry paneer cubes in it until golden brown.


Step-2

Remove the paneer cubes and put cumin, when it splutters, add bay leaf.Palak Paneer4.When it starts to crackle add ginger, garlic and onion paste. Cook until it gets pinkish-brown in color.


Step-3

Now, add tomato puree and stir fry over medium heat.Palak Paneer7.Add spinach and saute for 2-3 minutes.


Step-4

Now, add paneer cubes to the mix and turn around a few times to completely mix it with the palak gravy.Palak Paneer9.Pour some cream over, mix well and serve hot. .
