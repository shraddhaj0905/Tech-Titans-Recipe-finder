HOT-COFFEE

Ingredients



  • Instant Coffee Granules
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Hot Water
  • Jar With Tight-Fitting Lid

Recipe


Step-1

In a cup, take 1.5 teaspoons of instant coffee and 1.5 tablespoons sugar (or add as per taste).


Step-2

Add 3 tablespoons hot boiling water. You can heat water in an electric kettle or in a small pan. ¼ cup of water can also be added if you prefer a slightly weaker coffee flavor.


Step-3

First mix the coffee and sugar with water.. Then begin to stir briskly and beat coffee for 3 to 4 minutes.. Beat the coffee till its color lightens and you see a frothy layer on top.


Step-4

Pour the foamy milk in the cup.Serve the frothy cafe style hot coffee straightway.
