MIX-VEG

Ingredients



  • 3 tsp oil, 12 cubes paneer
  • 2 tbsp almonds, 1 potato
  • ½ carrot, ¼ cup peas
  • 4 beans, ¼ capsicum,
  • ½ cup cauliflower
  • 2 tomato, chopped, 1 inch cinnamon
  • 1 green chilli, 1 onion finely chopped
  • 4 tsp oil, 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt, 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cream / malai

Recipe


Step-1

firstly, saute 12 cubes paneer in 3 tsp of oil, and keep aside.in the same oil saute 2 tbsp blanched almonds till they turn golden brown.further, add 1 chopped potato, ½ carrot and saute for 3 minutes.also add ½ cup cauliflower, 4 beans and ¼ cup peas. saute for another 3 minutes.keep all the vegetables aside.


Step-2

now prepare the curry by heating 4 tsp oil and saute 1 bay leaf, 1 tsp cumin, 2 tsp kasuri methi and 1 green chillisaute 1 onion and 1 tsp ginger garlic paste till they turn slightly golden brown.


Step-3

further add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chilli powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, ¼ tsp garam masala and 1 tsp salt.saute for a minute or till spices get cooked completely.


Step-4

now prepare tomato paste by taking 2 chopped tomato in a blender.also add 1 inch cinnamon, 5 clove, 2 pods cardamom and 12 blanched almonds.blend to smooth paste without adding any extra water.


Step-5

transfer the prepared tomato puree to kadai and mix well.cover and cook for 5 minutes or till oil is released from masala paste.lower the flame and add ½ cup whisked curd. stir continuously, without allowing curd to curdle.


Step-6

now add in roasted mix vegetables and mix well.add ½ cup water or as required and mix well adjusting consistency.cover and continue to cook for another 10 minutes, or till vegetables are cooked completely. mixed vegetable curry


Step-7

turn off the flame and add 2 tbsp cream, 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp coriander. mix well.


Step-8

finally, serve MIX VEGETABLE CURRY
