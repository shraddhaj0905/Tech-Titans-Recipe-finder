SANDWICH

Ingredients



  • 6 slices bread
  • butter to toast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic crushed
  • 1 medium sized onion finely chopped
  • 2 small carrots finely chopped
  • 1/2 small capsicum finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • salt to taste

Recipe


Step-1

Heat oil in a pan - add crushed garlic. Add onion along with salt, saute until golden.


Step-2

Add carrot and capsicum. Saute until it shrinks and raw smell leaves.


Step-3

Add red chilli powder and garam masala powder. Give a quick saute.


Step-4

Add mayo. Mix it well. Switch off.


Step-5

Add generous filling to a bread slice. Seal it with another slice.


Step-6

Heat butter on a dosa tawa. Place the prepared sandwich. Press with dosa flipper. Brush with butter on top.


Step-7

Flip over and toast until golden brown on both the sides. Remove and place on chopping board, slice into half and serve Veg Sandwich hot!
